About Me

Who Am I?

Hello, I'm Maël Boccardi,

A passionate junior Quantitative Analyst with nearly two years of experience in asset management, I specialize in Python-based quantitative modeling, econometrics, machine learning, and portfolio construction. I have hands-on experiences in developing and validating advanced financial models, leveraging data science to address complex investment and risk management challenges. After recently completing the CFA Level I program at Université Paris Dauphine, I am eager to further deepen my skills in systematic risk management, deep learning, and advanced portfolio analytics.

My professional journey includes roles at Natixis Investment Managers and Ofi Invest Asset Management, where I contributed to systematic allocation, risk parity strategies, model validation, and macroeconomic regime-based portfolio construction. I have designed and implemented stress-testing frameworks and dynamic allocation strategies, consistently aiming to bridge quantitative research and practical asset management.

With a Master’s in Quantitative Finance, a Magistere in economics, and experience across both research and industry, I bring a robust technical toolkit and an analytical mindset to solving the financial sector’s challenging problems.
